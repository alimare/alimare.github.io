Alimar Enterainment
A software, video and media company. Originally started by Alimar Zerahan and Eric Ricks. It's now ran solely by Alimar. However, the future is brighter than ever better before!

Background

Starting in 1995, with the first release of a text-based game called Xavieria, Alimar Entertainment was born. Alimar Entertaiment's goal is to create entertianing media. Whether it be videos, books, or software, we promote original talent because we believe that everyone has a story and a dream to share. Good dreams are worth following. And that is Alimar Entertainment... nothing more or less than one man's dream realized.
